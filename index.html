About K&M Automotive

Honest Work.
Real Results.

K&M Automotive is a full-service auto shop on Chicago's Northwest Side, led by Ahmed — a mechanic who takes the time to properly diagnose your vehicle before ever picking up a wrench. We don't do work that isn't needed.

We've earned the trust of Chicago drivers through transparent pricing, quality craftsmanship, and genuine care. We cover auto body collision repair, full mechanical maintenance, and complex electrical systems — all under one roof so you're never bounced between shops.

From brake lines on a '96 Sable that two other shops refused to touch, to tracing down intermittent electrical faults — we don't walk away from difficult jobs.
